3.2.8. Using spin-boxes

Spin-boxes allow you to specify a real or integer number. (see Figure 3.7)

Figure 3.7. Example spin-box

Set the value of a spin-box by clicking the left mouse button on the up arrow to increase the value or on the down arrow to decrease the value. You may click and hold on the up or down arrow to move through the spin values quickly. You may also click on the text and type in a new value.
