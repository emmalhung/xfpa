3.4.4. Modifying with the "Sculpt" tool

The "Sculpt" tool allows you to modify a line or outline interactively.

  1. Choose the size of the sculpting tool (the "puck" size).

    The cursor appears as concentric circles while inside the map area.

  2. Choose a smoothing mode.

  3. Press and hold the left mouse button.

  4. As you move the puck along the line or outline, the shape changes to fit the edge of the puck.

  5. Release the left button when you have finished, or when the puck loses contact with the line or outline.

    The modified shape is displayed.

    Note: The smoothing parameter controls the extent of smoothing which occurs when the "Sculpt" tool is used. The smoothing is indicated by a second circle around the sculpting puck. The area between the circle and the puck is where smoothing occurs.
