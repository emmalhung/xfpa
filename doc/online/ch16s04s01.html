16.4.1. Animating your depictions

You can animate your depictions to view the pattern. You can animate the basic depictions or the interpolated depictions.

To animate your depiction:

  1. Click the Animation tab. (see Figure 16.18)

    Figure 16.18. Animation menus

  2. Select Depictions or Interpolations as the Source.

  3. From the list in the Animation area, select the fields to animate.

  4. Move the Time Span sliders to set the start and end times for the animation. (see Using slider-bars, (Section 3.3.5))

  5. Move the Speed slider to the left or right to set the speed of the animation. (see Using slider-bars, (Section 3.3.5))

  6. Click Start.

    The depiction automatically starts animating until you click Stop. However, you can change the source, fields and speed while it is running.
