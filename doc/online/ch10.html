The continuous field editor allows you to edit gridded fields in your depiction. Here are some examples of different continuous fields:

  • MSL pressure

  • surface temperature

  • wave height

  • sea temperature

  • maximum temperature

The continuous field editor controls appear when you choose to edit a continuous field.
