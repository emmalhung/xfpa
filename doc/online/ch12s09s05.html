12.9.5. Changing the template for an area or subarea

To redefine the value of an area or the value of a subarea of a divided area:

  1. Select the Modify tool.

  2. Pick inside the area or subarea to modify. (see Pick an object, (Section 9.4.1))

    The boundary turns yellow and the area or subarea is covered with a white grid.

  3. Change the values by selecting a template from the Memory section, or modify the values by clicking the right mouse button and selecting Set. (see Setting area values, (Section 12.1.3))

  4. Click the right mouse button and select Cancel to complete the edit and deselect the area.
