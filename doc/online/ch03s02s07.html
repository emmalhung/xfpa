3.2.7. Using drop-down and pop-up lists

Drop-down (Figure 3.5) and pop-up (Figure 3.6) lists allow you to chose from a preset list of options.

Figure 3.5. Example drop-down list

To use a drop-down list:

  1. Click the left mouse button on the icon to the right-hand side of the list button. The list appears.

  2. Click the left mouse button on the item you wish to select.

You may also click on the list button, then hold and drag the mouse. Release the mouse button while over the item you wish to select. If the mouse is not over the list when you release the mouse button, then no selection is made.

Figure 3.6. Example pop-up list

To use a pop-up list:

  1. Click the left mouse button anywhere on the list button. The list appears.

  2. Click the left mouse button on the item you wish to select.

You may also click on the list button, then hold and drag the mouse. Release the mouse button while over the item you wish to select. If the mouse is not over the list when you release the mouse button, then no selection is made.
