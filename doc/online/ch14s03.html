14.3. Moving a line

To move one or more lines.

  1. Select the Move tool.

  2. Pick the line(s) to move. (see Pick an object, (Section 9.4.1))

  3. Click the right mouse button and select Translate.

    The picked lines turn blue.

  4. Pick a reference point on the map and hold the left mouse button down.

    The reference point can be anywhere; however, if you are moving a portion of the line(s) off the depiction it is important to pick a point that will remain on the depiction after the move. The lines shimmer indicating you can now move them.

  5. Drag the line(s) to the new location and release the button.

    The FPA then recomputes the field and re-displays the new line(s) in white.

Note: The lines remain selected until you deselect them, so you may continue to use the Move tools. (see Unpick an object, (Section 9.4.2))
