6.11. Showing or hiding the guidance fields

To display the fields you selected in the guidance list in the active map area:

  1. Select the guidance fields from a guidance field list.

  2. Choose Guidance → Show Selected Fields.

To turn off the display:

  1. Choose Guidance → Hide Fields.

There is also an option in the Field Display Control to turn guidance on and off. (see Displaying fields, (Section 4.3.1))
