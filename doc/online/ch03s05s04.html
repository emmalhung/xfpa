3.5.4. Exiting the Zoom feature

When you have finished viewing your magnified depiction, you can exit from the Zoom function and return to your original view.

To exit the zoom feature:

  1. Choose Actions → Zoom → Exit Zoom.

    Your original depiction view appears.

    or

    Click the icon from the "Command Icon Bar" until you reach your original view. The icon will become inactive when you are no longer in Zoom mode.
