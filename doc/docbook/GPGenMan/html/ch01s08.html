1.8. Examples of Product Definition Files

Examples of product definition files for the PSMet, SVGMet and TexMet applications can be found in the directories:

$FPA/setup/pdf/psmet/examples
$FPA/setup/pdf/svgmet/examples
$FPA/setup/pdf/texmet/examples

Users may copy any portion of these files to their local directories to develop their own graphics products.

Warning

The files in these directories may be overwritten by future revisions of FPA, so that any editing should be carried out on local copies of these files.
