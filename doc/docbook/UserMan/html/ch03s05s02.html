3.5.2. Moving around (panning) in a depiction

After you magnify an image, use the Pan Mode of the Zoom function to move around (pan) magnified areas of a zoom. When you pan a map, you choose the centre of a window that slides around the magnified image.

To move around in a magnified depiction:

  1. After you have used the Zoom In function, choose Actions → Zoom → Pan Mode or click the icon from the "Command Icon Bar".

    The cursor changes to an arrow cross.

  2. Click in the centre of your new viewing window.

    The display moves in the direction that you chose. The point that you selected becomes the centre.

  3. Use the scroll bars to pan around a zoomed depiction image.

  4. When you are finished panning, click the right mouse button and select End Pan Mode.
