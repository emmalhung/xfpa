12.5. Moving an area

To move one or more areas.

  1. Select the Move Area tool.

    The move menus appear. (see Figure 12.6)

    Figure 12.6. Move menus

  2. Select Include Labels if you want to include labels.

  3. Pick the area(s) to move. (see Pick an object, (Section 9.4.1))

  4. Click the right mouse button and select Translate.

    The picked areas turn blue.

  5. Pick a reference point on the map and hold the left mouse button down.

    The reference point can be anywhere; however, if you are moving a portion of the area(s) off the depiction it is important to pick a point that will remain on the depiction after the move. The area boundaries shimmer indicating you can now move them.

  6. Drag the feature to the new location and release the button.

    The FPA then recomputes the field and re-displays the new areas in white.

Note: The areas remain selected until you deselect them, so you may continue to use the Move tools. (see Unpick an object, (Section 9.4.2))
