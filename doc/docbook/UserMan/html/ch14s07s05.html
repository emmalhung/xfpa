14.7.5. Breaking lines

To break one line into two lines:

  1. Select the Join tool.

  2. Pick a line to break. (see Pick an object, (Section 9.4.1))

    The picked line turns yellow.

  3. Click the right mouse button and select Break.

  4. Pick a break point on the line.
