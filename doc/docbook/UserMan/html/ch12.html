The area editor allows you to draw and manipulate discrete areas on your depiction. These areas may overlap.

This includes fields such as:

  • system weather

  • probability of precipitation

  • ice cover

  • wind (adjustment area)

    Note: Wind areas are a special case. You can define how to calculate a wind rather than setting the wind itself.

The area editor controls appear when you choose to edit a discrete field. (see Figure 12.1)
