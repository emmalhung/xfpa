12.10.5. Deleting an area hole

To delete an area hole:

  1. Select the Modify tool.

  2. Pick the area hole to delete. (see Pick an object, (Section 9.4.1))

    The hole boundary turns yellow.

  3. Click the right mouse button and select Delete Hole.
