5.7. Printing a depiction

You can choose to print the map area (the active depiction plus the static or daily fields with guidance showing) to an output destination such as a laser printer or file.

  1. Choose Fields → Print Active.

    The Destination menu appears to the right.

  2. Select the destination. The cursor turns into an hourglass until the printing is complete.
