17.2. Displaying products status

To display the status of all available products:

  1. Choose Products → Status... or click the clock icon in the upper right-hand corner of the screen.

    The Product Status dialog box appears listing all the products available. The status displays "No Previous Run", "Running" or the time at which the product was last generated.
