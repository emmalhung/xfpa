6.1. Checking guidance status

You can check the last update date and time for all of the guidance sources.

To check guidance status:

  1. Choose Guidance → Status or click the icon in the upper right-hand corner of your screen.

    The Guidance Status dialog box appears. (see Figure 6.3)

    Note: The bookshelf icon flashes when new source is coming in. The bookshelf icon stops flashing after you check the status.

    Figure 6.3. Guidance Status

  2. Click Close.
