A.9. Samples block

A.9.1. Template of a 'Samples' Block

The 'Samples' block is used to identify internal models used to sample parameters or winds from a given field. Note that each value_samptype or wind_function refers to an FPA routine to determine parameters or wind speed and direction based on a given field.
