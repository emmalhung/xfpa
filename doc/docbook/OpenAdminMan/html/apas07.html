A.7. Groups block

A.7.1. Template of a 'Groups' Block

The Groups block is used to identify grouping of fields and elements in the FPA interface. The 'Fields' group_name "Not_Displayed" identifies fields that will not appear in the interface, the 'Elements' group_name "Not_Displayed" identifies elements that will not appear in the interface, and the 'Elements' group_name, "Generic_Equation" identifies internally accessed equations that will also not appear in the interface.
